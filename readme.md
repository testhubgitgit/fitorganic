### 👌 Collagen Berry Smoothie

**High-protein, skin-friendly recovery drink** –

#### Ingredients (1 serving)
- 1 scoop collagen protein powder (unflavored or vanilla)
- ½ frozen banana (for creaminess)
- ½ cup frozen berries (strawberries, blueberries, or mixed)
- 4–5 tbsp plain Greek yogurt (for creaminess & extra protein)
- 1 tsp chia seeds
- ½ tsp cinnamon
- Tiny pinch of salt (for electrolytes)
- 3–4 ice cubes
- 50–80 ml cold water (only if needed to blend)

#### Instructions
1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
2. Blend until smooth and thick (30–45 seconds). Add a splash of water only if too thick.
3. Enjoy right away.

#### Benefits
- ~25g+ protein  
- Collagen supports skin healing & scar improvement  
- Greek yogurt + chia for probiotics, fiber & creaminess  
- Berries for antioxidants  
- Low-bloat & refreshing


Enjoy! 💚
